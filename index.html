Impact Entrance Scholarship Application

Dave Litman


Have you ever felt trapped? I’m not talking about that bumper-to-bumper rush hour commute, or being in an elevator stuck between floors… I’m talking about really feeling trapped in your life circumstances. It seems that all doors are shut. What if you graduated high school, and chose a career path based on the available avenues of education, and skill sets available at the time? Then just as your chosen career in TV writing and production is taking off, and you’re starting a family, and essentially, living your life, along comes a new, hitherto unheard of technology. A technology that’s going to change the world. A technology that you could only dream to be a part of, since you’re locked in to your profession, and you need to keep going to support your young new family. It’s too late to look back now. You’re stuck. You’re trapped.

Or so you thought.

What if I told you that there is a second chance awaiting you? New doors to open, a second career that’s possible, if you apply yourself, and share your “first life” knowledge along the way… A second chance. This is how I look at the world of technology… The world of the internet, and the World Wide Web. In the mid-90s, just over 20 years ago, when the world started to become connected via URLs and websites and online forums, I was already set in my chosen career of TV production, but I saw a great new world opening up that I always knew would be drawing me in. I just didn’t know when. Because, after all, I was trapped.

Or so I thought.

Now, with a chance to reassess life, and my professional dreams, I realize that I actually can seize an opportunity, and learn tech. Learn web development and design. Imagine the excitement of starting with a blank slate, and creating a colourful, dynamic, exciting, functioning website. From scratch. The thought of it is exhilarating. In the same way I used to think that creating TV programs was inspiring, now, in the 21st century, it’s the online world, up in the clouds, that stirs those emotions.

With an education at RED Academy, not only can I change my own career and dreams, but I can help inspire others too. You don’t have to be “stuck” anymore. If you see something that inspires you professionally, and you want to fulfill those dreams, then take a chance. Go for it. You’ll never know what you can accomplish until you try. It may seem daunting, to be sure. I have those doubts too. But you don’t want to be left one day with a “what if I had tried that?”, lingering and haunting you.
I would love to bring this message to the community, and inspire others. Anyone can reach their dreams, if they just take a chance and open new doors. Inspire yourself to reach for your dreams. For me, it all starts with RED Academy.
